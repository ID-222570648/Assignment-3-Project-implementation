About the company


My Fruits and Veggies is a family owned company. We are opering from 1980 and now it is the biggest fruits and vegitable shop in the area. In 1980 when the business started, our ethical values for food made the business unique. We are now the biggest supplier of organic and non organic fruits and vegitables in Victoria.

What we do


We sell organic and non-organic fruits and vegitables to local customers and to the markets around Victoria. We grow at our own garden and we also get supply from the quality growers including organic growers. We try our best to keep our customers happy.
